About

I currently work as a Recruitment Consultant at Softtest Pays, a Canberra-based ICT staffing agency, where I run end-to-end hiring for Australian government contracts — sourcing, screening, candidate engagement, and security clearance coordination for Baseline and NV1 roles. I've screened 500+ resumes and supported 50+ clearance cases, all while keeping documentation audit-ready for submission to government stakeholders.

That's where compliance and recruitment overlap: checking documents against fixed standards, writing things down precisely, and not letting ambiguity slide. It's the part of the job I gravitate toward, and it's pulling me toward Governance, Risk & Compliance as a formal next step. I'm building the technical foundation for that move now, starting with CompTIA Security+.
